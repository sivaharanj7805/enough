## Every Element. What It Means. Why It Matters.

## 🗺️ THE MAP (Your Content Base)

Your ecosystem IS your base. Like Clash of Clans, everything on the map represents something real about your content. Nothing is decorative.

---

## 🌍 TERRAIN / ZONES (= Topic Clusters)

Each zone is a **content cluster** — a group of posts about the same topic.

| Biome | Health Range | Visual | What It Means |
|-------|-------------|--------|---------------|
| 🌲 **Forest** | 70-100 | Lush green, tall trees, deer, sunshine | This topic is THRIVING. Strong traffic, good SEO, well-linked. Your stronghold. |
| 🌻 **Meadow** | 50-70 | Yellow-green, medium trees, bees | Healthy but room to grow. Like a village that needs more buildings. |
| 🌱 **Seedbed** | 40-55 | Light green, small new trees, fog | New/growing topic. Recently planted content. Needs nurturing. |
| 🪴 **Swamp** | 25-50 | Orange/brown, sick trees, foxes, storm | DANGER. Posts are cannibalizing each other. Traffic declining. Needs consolidation. |
| 🏜️ **Desert** | 0-25 | Grey/brown, dead stumps, vultures | Critical. Thin or dead content. Losing rankings. Rebuild or abandon. |

**Zone border glow** = biome boundary. Soft gradient edges blend between zones.

**Zone label** = cluster name (AI-generated from post topics: "API Integration", "Getting Started")

**Health badge** = colored number (green/yellow/red) showing cluster health score

## 🌳 TREES (= Blog Posts)

Every single tree is a specific blog post. No exceptions.

| Tree Type | Role | Visual | What It Means |
|-----------|------|--------|---------------|
| 🌳 **Oak (tall, full canopy)** | **Pillar** | Tallest tree, rich green leaves, thick trunk | The anchor post of the cluster. Highest traffic, most links. Your main piece. |
| 🌿 **Birch (medium)** | **Supporter** | Medium height, blue-tinted leaves | Supporting post that links to the pillar. Drives secondary traffic. |
| ⚔️ **Thorn tree (twisted)** | **Competitor** | Orange/red leaves, crooked branches | This post COMPETES with another in the same cluster. It's hurting you. |
| 🪵 **Dead stump** | **Dead Weight** | Short, grey, few/no leaves | Post that gets no traffic, adds no value. Taking up crawl budget. |

**Tree SIZE** = traffic volume (bigger tree = more monthly visits)
**Tree LEAVES** = content freshness (lush = recent, sparse = stale)
**Tree HEIGHT** = word count / depth (taller = more comprehensive)

**Hover any tree →** see: post title, URL, traffic/mo, health score, role, age, word count
**Click any tree →** zoom in, see AI recommendations, "View Post" button

## 🐾 CREATURES (= User Behavior Metrics)

Animals roam the landscape based on real analytics data. Each species = a specific metric.

| Creature | Metric | Good/Bad | What It Means |
|----------|--------|----------|---------------|
| 🦌 **Deer** | Time-on-page | ✅ GOOD | Readers are deeply engaged. Avg 3-5 min reading. High-quality content. |
| 🐝 **Bees** | Backlinks | ✅ GOOD | Other sites are linking to this content. Building authority. Bees = earning citations. |
| 🦋 **Butterflies** | Social shares | ✅ GOOD | Content being shared on social media. Spreading organically. |
| 🐇 **Rabbits** | New visitors | ✅ GOOD | Attracting new organic traffic. Growing audience. |
| 🦊 **Foxes** | Bounce rate | ❌ BAD | Visitors land and immediately leave. Content doesn't match search intent. |
| 🦅 **Birds** | Low CTR | ⚠️ WARNING | Lots of impressions in search but nobody clicks. Titles/descriptions need work. |
| 🦃 **Vultures** | Ranking drops | ❌ BAD | Keywords losing positions. Competitors overtaking you. Content decaying. |
| 🐍 **Snakes** | Toxic backlinks | ❌ BAD | Spammy sites linking to you. Could trigger penalty. |

**Creature COUNT** = severity (3 foxes = worse bounce rate than 1 fox)
**Creature BEHAVIOR:**
- Deer graze peacefully near healthy trees
- Bees swarm around trees earning links (with visible hive at high levels)
- Foxes skulk around zone edges (visitors leaving)
- Vultures circle overhead (rankings falling)
- Butterflies float between trees (content being shared)

## 🌊 RIVERS (= Internal Link Flow)

Rivers connect clusters. They represent the internal linking between topic groups.

| River Quality | Visual | What It Means |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| ✨ **Sparkling** | Bright blue, wide, fast flow particles | Strong bidirectional linking. 10+ links. Excellent site architecture. |
| 💧 **Clear** | Medium blue, steady flow | Good linking. 5-10 links. Healthy connection. |
| 🟤 **Murky** | Brown/green, narrow, slow flow | Weak linking. 1-4 links. Could be stronger. |
| ☠️ **Toxic** | Dark green, thin, sickly glow | Problematic links. Broken, one-directional, or link to dead content. |

**River WIDTH** = number of internal links between clusters
**Flow PARTICLES** = link direction (particles flow the way links point)
**Hover river →** see: exact link count, quality assessment, which pages link where

---

## 🍄 MUSHROOMS (= Duplicate/Cannibalized Content)

Mushrooms grow where duplicate content festers. They're warning signs.

| Visual | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| Red cap with white spots | Two or more posts are near-duplicates (70%+ content similarity) |
| Pulsing red glow | Active cannibalization — posts competing for same keywords on Google |
| Size = severity | Bigger mushroom = higher similarity % = worse problem |
| Label shows similarity % | "89% similar" — nearly identical content |

**Hover mushroom →** see: which exact posts are duplicates, similarity score, traffic being wasted
**What to do:** Consolidate (merge posts), redirect the weaker one to the stronger one

---

## 🪨 BOULDERS (= Broken Links / Technical Issues)

Boulders block the landscape where technical problems exist.

| Visual | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| Grey rock with crack lines | Broken internal or external link |
| Orange glow = high severity | Many clicks being lost |
| Red glow = critical | Major broken link affecting SEO |
| Crack pattern = damage type | 404 (dead), 301 loop (redirect chain), timeout |

**Hover boulder →** see: exact broken URL, HTTP status, monthly clicks lost, severity
**What to do:** Fix the link, set up proper redirect, or remove the link

# THE CLASH OF CLANS MODEL

## Why People Open Clash of Clans Every Day

1. **Something is always building/upgrading** — you come back to check if it's done
2. **Resources accumulate** — gold/elixir pile up, you need to spend them
3. **You can be attacked** — urgency to defend
4. **There's always a next upgrade** — endless progression
5. **It's YOUR base** — personal ownership, pride
6. **Social proof** — clan wars, leaderboards, showing off

## How Tended Mirrors This

| CoC Element | Tended Equivalent | Implementation |
|-------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Your base** | Your ecosystem landscape | Unique to every user, reflects THEIR content |
| **Buildings** | Trees (blog posts) | Each post is a "building" you can upgrade |
| **Upgrading** | Improving posts (update content, add links) | "Upgrade" a post → tree grows visibly |
| **Resource collection** | Traffic accumulation | Watch traffic numbers grow as you improve |
| **Being attacked** | Competitor movement / algorithm updates | "Storm incoming: competitor published similar content" |
| **Defending** | Content freshening / updating | Update stale content before rankings drop |
| **Attacking** | Publishing new content to compete | "Plant a new tree" to capture a keyword |
| **Builder queue** | Quest queue | 3-5 active quests at any time, always something to do |
| **Clash currency (gems)** | Content health points | Spend effort → earn health points |
| **Clan** | Team / company | Team members see the same landscape, work together |
| **Leaderboard** | Industry benchmarks | "Your ecosystem ranks #12 in SaaS content" |
| **War map** | Competitor overlay | See competitor's "base" overlaid on yours |
| **Achievements** | Easter eggs + badges | Unicorn, Phoenix, Dragon, Crown |
| **Daily login reward** | Streak counter | 🔥 Come back daily to maintain streak |

## The Daily Loop

```
Morning: Open Tended
  → See: "☀️ Your API forest grew overnight (+120 visits)"
  → See: "⛈️ Storm warning: competitor published 'OAuth Guide'"  
  → See: "🗡️ Quest available: Consolidate Getting Started swamp"
  → See: "🔥 Day 13 streak — don't break it!"
  → See: "🍄 New duplicate detected in Security cluster"

Action: Pick a quest, do the work

Evening: Check back
  → See: "🌱 Your consolidated post is taking root"
  → See: "🔥 Day 13 maintained — 1 day to Silver badge!"
  → See: Ecosystem health: 67 → 68 (+1)

Weekly: Content Wrapped arrives
  → "You planted 2 trees, drained 1 swamp, earned 3 backlinks (bees)"
  → Share card on LinkedIn → colleagues see it → "what app is this?"
```

## The Key Insight

**Clash of Clans never tells you "congratulations, you're done."**
There is ALWAYS a next upgrade, a next defense, a next attack.

Tended should work the same way:
- Finished consolidation? → "New quest: add links to your rebuilt pillar"
- Hit 80 health? → "Quest: reach 90 for the Crown achievement"
- All clusters healthy? → "New: competitor overlay unlocked — compare your base"
- Won the keyword? → "Defend it: 2 competitors targeting this term"

**The game never ends because content strategy never ends.**
