🌍 Terrain Zones = Topic Clusters

🌲 Forest 70-100

VisualLush green, tall trees, sunshine overhead, deer grazing
MeaningThis topic cluster is thriving. Strong traffic, good rankings, well-linked.
ActionProtect it. Keep content fresh. Add supporting posts.

🌻 Meadow 50-70

VisualYellow-green, medium trees, bees buzzing
MeaningHealthy but room to grow. Needs more depth or more supporting content.
ActionAdd supporting posts. Strengthen internal links.

🌱 Seedbed 40-55

VisualLight green, small trees, fog, quiet
MeaningNew/growing topic. Recently planted content. Not enough data yet.
ActionNurture with more content. Be patient. Add links from forests.

🪴 Swamp 25-50

VisualOrange/brown, sick trees, foxes, storm clouds, mushrooms
MeaningDANGER. Posts cannibalizing each other. Traffic declining. Content confusion.
ActionConsolidate! Merge overlapping posts into one strong pillar.

🏜️ Desert 0-25

VisualGrey/brown, dead stumps, vultures circling, boulders
MeaningCritical. Thin or dead content. No traffic. Losing rankings.
ActionRebuild with quality content or redirect and abandon.
🌳 Trees = Individual Blog Posts

🌳 Pillar (Oak)

VisualTallest tree, rich green canopy, thick trunk
MeaningThe anchor post of the cluster. Highest traffic, most comprehensive.
Size =Monthly traffic volume
Leaves =Content freshness (lush = recent)
Height =Word count / depth

🌿 Supporter (Birch)

VisualMedium height, blue-tinted leaves
MeaningSupporting post that links to the pillar. Drives secondary traffic.
GoalLink these TO the pillar. They strengthen the cluster.

⚔️ Competitor (Thorn)

VisualOrange/red leaves, twisted branches
MeaningThis post COMPETES with another in the same cluster. Hurting both.
ActionMerge into the stronger post. Redirect. Stop the bleeding.

🪵 Dead Weight (Stump)

VisualShort, grey, sparse/no leaves
MeaningGets no traffic. Wastes crawl budget. Adds nothing.
ActionRedirect to pillar or delete. Free up the space.
🐾 Creatures = User Behavior Metrics

🦌 Deer GOOD

MetricTime-on-page (high engagement)
MeaningReaders spending 3-5+ minutes. Content is deeply engaging.
BehaviorGraze peacefully near healthy trees
EvolutionFawn → Deer → Stag with antlers (as engagement grows)

🐝 Bees GOOD

MetricBacklinks earned
MeaningOther sites citing this content. Building authority.
BehaviorSwarm around trees earning links
EvolutionSingle bee → Swarm → Hive on tree

🦋 Butterflies GOOD

MetricSocial shares
MeaningContent spreading on social media. Organic reach.
BehaviorFloat between trees, colorful

🦊 Foxes BAD

MetricBounce rate (high)
MeaningVisitors land and immediately leave. Content doesn't match intent.
BehaviorSkulk around zone edges
CountMore foxes = worse bounce rate

🦅 Birds WARNING

MetricLow click-through rate (CTR)
MeaningShowing up in search but nobody clicks. Titles need work.
BehaviorCircle above zone

🦃 Vultures BAD

MetricRanking drops
MeaningKeywords losing positions. Competitors overtaking you.
BehaviorCircle high above dying zones
🌊 Rivers + 🍄 Features + ☁️ Weather

🌊 Rivers = Internal Links

✨ SparklingStrong links (10+), wide, bright blue
💧 ClearGood links (5-10), steady flow
🟤 MurkyWeak links (1-4), narrow, brown
☠️ ToxicBroken/problematic links, dark green
Width =Number of links between clusters
Particles =Direction of link flow

🍄 Mushrooms = Duplicates

VisualRed-capped mushroom with pulsing glow
MeaningTwo posts are near-duplicates (70%+)
Size =Similarity % (bigger = more duplicate)
LabelShows which posts + similarity %
ActionConsolidate into one post + redirect

🪨 Boulders = Broken Links

VisualGrey rock with orange/red glow + cracks
MeaningA URL is returning 404, 301 loop, or timeout
Color =🔴 Critical, 🟠 High, 🟡 Medium severity
ActionFix the link or set up proper redirect
